Management Philosophy (The Meaning of Our Work)

Why We Work?

Everyone has their reasons for working, often to gain experience, knowledge, or money. However, we believe in working to "give" rather than "get." This means providing value to society and our customers, as true professionals do. Our focus is on contributing to others rather than solely benefiting ourselves.

What We Give is What We Get

People often work hard to get something but fail because they focus on receiving rather than giving. If you don't give to your customers and society, you won't receive in return. Self-centered thinking and blaming others won't help. Understand that you can't get more than you give. By consistently giving, you will eventually receive money, knowledge, and experience. Following this philosophy, we commit to contributing to society through our customers.

What is Success?

We usually think of successful people as having money, wealth, or social status. And I do not think it's necessary to work so hard to succeed. What we see is what successful people get. But we need to know the essence of why successful people get it. A successful person is someone who continues to add value to society.
